# Area 2: Ravine Wagon Wreck

> The ravine smells of blood, wet clay, and sharp alchemical bitterness. A wagon lies split open below, its goods burst across the stone. White powder clings to wheel spokes and mule hides, and the dead nearby have not simply been killed — they have been enthusiastically torn apart.

**Referee Information:** Three dead smugglers lie here, all savaged. The party can find 8 doses of white plume dust, concealed valuables, and coded notes about a rendezvous at the logging camp.
