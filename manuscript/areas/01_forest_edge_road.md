# Area 1: Forest Edge Road

> The road ends not with a gate, but with hesitation. Broken campfires, trampled mud, and the stink of damp canvas lie at the forest's edge. Beyond, the trees knit themselves into a dark green wall, and somewhere within comes the sharp, wet sound of wood being split.

**Referee Information:** Here the party may meet Elnara and any surviving locals. Tracks show something heavy moving in and out of the treeline.
