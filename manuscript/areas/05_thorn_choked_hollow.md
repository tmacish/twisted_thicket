# Area 5: Thorn-Choked Hollow

> The hollow is a butcher's midden of snapped branches and pale-streaked bones. Something huge moves just beyond the thorn wall with a wet huffing sound, and for a heartbeat the air smells not like beast, but like a wizard's workshop after a fire.

**Referee Information:** This is the lair of the White Plume Owlbear.

---

## White Plume Owlbear

**Frequency:** Unique &nbsp; **No. Appearing:** 1  
**AC:** 3 &nbsp; **Move:** 15" &nbsp; **HD:** 8+3  
**% in Lair:** 95% &nbsp; **Treasure Type:** C, incidental special  
**No. of Attacks:** 3 &nbsp; **Damage/Attack:** 1d8+3 / 1d8+3 / 2d6+3  
**Special Attacks:** Hug, Dust Frenzy  
**Special Defenses:** Nil &nbsp; **Magic Resistance:** Standard  
**Intelligence:** Animal (disturbed) &nbsp; **Alignment:** Neutral  
**Size:** L &nbsp; **Psionic Ability:** Nil

### Special Abilities

**Hug:** If both claw attacks hit the same target in a round, the owlbear clutches the victim and automatically inflicts 2d6 extra damage in the following round unless the victim escapes.

**Dust Frenzy:** Three times per day, the creature may enter a frenzy at the start of its turn. For 1 round it gains +2 to hit, +2 damage, and one additional claw attack. At the end of that round it suffers 1d4 damage.

**K-Whispers:** When the owlbear enters Dust Frenzy, all creatures within 30 feet must save versus spells or suffer a −1 penalty to attack rolls and saving throws for 1 round.
