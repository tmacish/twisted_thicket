# Area 3: Logging Camp

> The logging camp looks as if a storm developed teeth. Tarps hang in strips from poles, split logs are scattered through the mud, and deep claw marks run up the watchtower supports. A cookfire still smolders under a pot kicked half over in the panic.

**Referee Information:** The survivors saw the beast strike in a frenzy, then vanish into the trees with impossible speed. This is the best place for negotiation or a short fight with smugglers loyal to the false inheritor.
