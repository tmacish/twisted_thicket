# Area 7: Riddle Cave of the Ash Tongue

> The cave mouth is little more than a split in the earth, black around the edges as if some ancient fire once breathed from it. Inside, the stone throat widens into a chamber no larger than a cottage, its walls scratched with old symbols, soot marks, and berry-red handprints.

---

## The Riddle

> *When white breath wears a mortal face,*  
> *Seek not the height but hollow place.*  
> *Turn the eye and still the tongue,*  
> *There lies the path for old and young.*

**Solution:** Turn the carved eye clockwise, then press and hold the tongue-shaped stone in silence for one full round.

---

## Reward

A hidden drawer reveals:

- A bark-map
- 2 doses of white plume dust
- A gnome warning (written in Gnomish)
- A marked obsidian token
