# Area 4: Dust Pool Glade

> A pale sheen lies over the glade as though moonlight has pooled on the ground before sunset. At its center, a shallow basin of water shines white-blue, and the smell here is cold, bitter, and faintly sweet — wrong for any honest woodland spring.

**Referee Information:** A cleansing rite here can destroy the tainted pool and grant the party a small edge against the owlbear's mind-rattling frenzy.
