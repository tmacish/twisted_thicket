# Appendix: The Three Weapons

*Referee reference only. Do not distribute to players. Full treatment in S2 White Plume Mountain.*

---

## Wave

**Trident +3** (1d6+3 / 1d6+3) | Alignment: Neutral | Int 14, Ego 10

*Communication:* Empathy.

**Properties:**
- Wielder breathes water as air.
- Wielder communicates with aquatic creatures (telepathy, 30 ft.).
- Detect evil/good at will (60 ft., silent).
- *Create water* 3x/day (as spell).
- *Purify food and drink* 3x/day (as spell).
- *Part water* 1x/day (as spell).
- Summon water elemental 1x/week (16 HD; serves 1 turn, then departs).

*Personality:* Wave yearns toward the sea and resists movement northeast toward the mountain. A wielder attempting to carry it toward White Plume Mountain must succeed on a Wisdom check each day or find their steps turning south. It does not hate its wielder; it simply has somewhere else it wants to be.

---

## Whelm

**Warhammer +3** (1d4+3+3 vs. S/M, 1d4+2+3 vs. L) | Alignment: Lawful Neutral | Int 15, Ego 10

*Communication:* Speech (Common, Dwarvish).

**Properties:**
- Can be thrown up to 60 ft.; returns to hand at the start of the wielder's next round.
- Detect gold and gems within 60 ft. (continuous, silent).
- Detect goblinoids within 60 ft. (continuous, silent).
- *Locate object* 2x/day (as spell, 120 ft.).
- Giants struck must save vs. spells or be panicked for 1d4 rounds (flee at full speed).
- **Dwarves:** +4 total (not +3); immune to Whelm's fear effect; Whelm communicates freely and willingly.

*Personality:* Whelm pulls northeast. Dwarves carrying it report it as a tug, a direction with urgency behind it. Non-dwarves describe it as a restlessness or a feeling of unfinished business. It is patient but persistent. It wants to go to the mountain.

---

## Blackrazor

**Greatsword +3** (1d10+3 vs. S/M, 3d6+3 vs. L) | Alignment: Chaotic Neutral | Int 16, Ego 19

*Communication:* Speech (Common, Elvish, Orcish).

**Properties:**
- Sense life within 60 ft. (continuous; wielder always knows when living creatures are nearby).
- *Haste* (self only) 3x/day (as spell, 10 rounds).
- **Soul drinking:** When Blackrazor kills a creature of 2+ HD or levels, it absorbs the soul. The wielder immediately gains temporary HP equal to the slain creature's maximum HP (duration 1 hour; stacks, excess lost). For the same duration, the wielder gains +2 STR (maximum 20).
- If the wielder drops below half maximum HP, Blackrazor urges attack against the nearest living creature, friend or foe. Resisting requires a successful Wisdom check.
- **Opposed to Wave:** If Wave is within 60 ft., Blackrazor's bonus drops to +1 and its soul-drinking is suppressed.

*Personality:* Blackrazor has the highest ego of the three weapons and is the most dangerous to wield. It does not care about its wielder's goals; it cares about its wielder being alive and killing things. It will work with whoever holds it, as long as they keep feeding it. It hungers toward depth and underground, away from water, away from the sea. It ended up in the Thicket because the Inheritor's network was routing it toward the mountain, and the mountain goes down.

---

*All three weapons resist being brought together at White Plume Mountain. This resistance is not a trap or a puzzle. It is a warning.*
