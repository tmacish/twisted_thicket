# Wandering Encounters

Check for a wandering encounter once per hour of travel or exploration, or whenever the party makes significant noise. Roll 1d6.

| d6 | Encounter |
|----|-----------|
| 1 | 1d4 giant weasels, agitated by trace dust |
| 2 | 2d4 smugglers or scavengers searching the trails |
| 3 | A maimed survivor fleeing blindly through the woods |
| 4 | The distant cry of the owlbear; all are surprised on 1–2 in the next encounter |
| 5 | A patch of white drifting dust, enough for one dose |
| 6 | No encounter |
