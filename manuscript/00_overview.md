# The Twisted Thicket

*SF1 -- An OSRIC / AD&D 2E compatible wilderness adventure for 4-6 player characters of levels 5-7*

---

## Adventure Background

North of the settled roadlands, beyond the last safe timber camps and the sodden tracks leading toward White Plume Mountain, lies a tangled woodland known as the Twisted Thicket. In recent weeks, merchants, trappers, and patrols have gone missing there. Mangled bodies, shattered wagons, and strange white powder at the scenes have become the talk of nearby taverns.

The truth is more dangerous than rumor. Agents of a false inheritor of the ancient wizard Keraptis have been smuggling a refined magical stimulant called **white plume dust** through the thicket. The powder is a failed byproduct of attempted thought-transfer magic, fitfully carrying traces of alien intellect and violent impulse. Months before the adventure begins, an early shipment broke open in a ravine and an adult owlbear devoured the scattered cargo. The creature survived, but its flesh and mind were transformed. When a more recent wagon wreck drew the owlbear back to the ravine by scent, the creature re-exposed itself through contact with scattered traces of the dust still present at the site, deepening the addiction and intensifying the corruption that has driven it into bouts of ecstatic fury ever since. Those who sample the dust experience strength, obsession, and flashes of a hostile intelligence.

This scenario is designed as a compact outdoor adventure that can be completed in a single long session or used as a prelude to a deeper expedition toward White Plume Mountain.

---

## Adventure Synopsis

The player characters are hired to enter the Twisted Thicket, discover the source of a series of brutal killings, and recover or destroy any contraband they find. Their search leads them through a wrecked smuggler wagon, a damaged logging camp, a corrupted glade, and finally into the thorn-choked hollow of the maddened owlbear. Along the way, they must decide whether to trust local wardens, bargain with smugglers, exploit the dust for power, or purge it at the source.
