# Hex Travel Map

A simple local hex map ties the road, forest sites, and northern approach together.

## Keyed Hexes

| Hex | Location |
|-----|----------|
| A | Road to Town |
| B | Forest Edge Camp |
| C | Ravine Wagon Wreck |
| D | Logging Camp |
| E | Dust Pool Glade |
| F | Thorn-Choked Hollow |
| G | Riddle Cave of the Ash Tongue |
| H | Northern Rise (sightline to White Plume Mountain) |

---

## Referee Use of the Hex Map

Run the map as a small decision space rather than a true long-range wilderness crawl.

- Put **Road to Town** and **Forest Edge Camp** in front of the players as obvious starts.
- Let **Ravine Wreck** act as the mandatory clue hex.
- Use **Dust Pool Glade**, **Riddle Cave**, and **Logging Camp** as choice-driven side hexes.
- Keep **Dead Gnoll's Eye Socket** as a fallback or sequel hook.
- Reveal **Northern Rise** only after the party has enough evidence to care about White Plume Mountain.

The road gives the cleanest southern entry, the Ravine Wreck is the core clue hex, the lateral hexes provide optional discovery and support, and Thorn-Choked Hollow is the likely endgame.
