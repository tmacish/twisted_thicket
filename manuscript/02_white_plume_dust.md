# White Plume Dust

White plume dust is a glittering, bitter-smelling powder packed in wax-sealed ceramic tubes or casks lined with bladder skin. It is dangerous but temptingly effective.

---

## Effects

A creature taking one dose as an action gains the following benefits for 10 rounds:

- +2 bonus to attack rolls
- +2 bonus to damage rolls
- +2 bonus to saving throws
- +2 bonus to morale checks, if applicable

---

## Backlash

At the end of the duration, the user must save versus spells. On a failed save, roll on the table below.

| d6 | Dust Backlash |
|----|---------------|
| 1 | The user mutters "I am Keraptis" or similarly grandiose nonsense for 1d6 rounds. |
| 2 | The user becomes obsessed with locating more dust and must spend 1d4 rounds moving toward the nearest visible cache. |
| 3 | The user suffers vivid visual distortions and takes a -1 penalty to attack rolls for 1 turn. |
| 4 | The user scratches crab-like sigils or the letter K into nearby surfaces whenever idle for the remainder of the expedition. |
| 5 | The user loses 1 point of Wisdom until after a full night of rest. |
| 6 | The user hears a hostile inner voice and must save again or lash out at the nearest ally for 1 round. |

---

## Multiple Doses

A creature that takes a second dose before the first expires gains a second independent duration of 10 rounds beginning immediately. At the end of each duration, the creature saves separately; each failed save produces its own backlash result, rolled independently on the table above. When two or more backlash results are active simultaneously, both apply ,  symptoms that conflict do not cancel, they stack. Taking three or more doses in rapid succession does not extend this further; each dose beyond the first adds one additional backlash save when the combined duration ends.
