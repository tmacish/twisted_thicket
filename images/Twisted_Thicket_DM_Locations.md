# DM Key: The Twisted Thicket (10x10 Mile Sector)

This document keys the locations from the DM map to the specific events and lore found in the **Twisted Thicket**.

## Primary Quest Locations (1–7)
These locations are the central nodes of the current investigation regarding the smuggling ring and the corrupted beast.

* **Area 1: Forest Edge Road** The threshold where the southern road dissolves into the Thicket. This is the site where the initial coach was struck and split [cite: 01_forest_edge_road.md].
  
* **Area 2: Ravine Wagon Wreck** A 15-foot drop-off where Coach B lies split along its spine [cite: 02_ravine_wagon_wreck.md]. This is where the alchemical "white powder" (contaminated dust) is present, and the warhammer **Whelm** can be recovered [cite: 02_ravine_wagon_wreck.md].
  
* **Area 3: Logging Camp** A cleared area featuring a bunkhouse, watchtower, and supply shed [cite: 03_logging_camp.md]. It is the site of a deadly encounter with the owlbear and the smuggler Sorel Vane [cite: 03_logging_camp.md].
  
* **Area 4: Dust Pool Glade** A clearing with a glowing white-blue basin that acts as a scrying conduit for a mysterious entity [cite: 04_dust_pool_glade.md]. **Old Brindle** is found here attempting a cleansing rite [cite: 04_dust_pool_glade.md].
  
* **Area 5: Thorn-Choked Hollow** The lair of the contaminated **White Plume Owlbear**, ringed by a wall of blackthorn [cite: 05_thorn_choked_hollow.md]. The hollow was prepared as a containment site for the beast [cite: 05_thorn_choked_hollow.md].
  
* **Area 6: Game Trails** A network of interconnected paths used by the beast, gnomes, and smugglers [cite: 06_game_trails.md]. The greatsword **Blackrazor** is found embedded in a white oak tree in this area [cite: 06_game_trails.md].
  
* **Area 7: Riddle Cave of the Ash Tongue** A rocky outcrop containing a hidden cache and a mechanism known as the "Ash Tongue Face" [cite: 07_riddle_cave_of_the_ash_tongue.md]. It provides navigation intelligence for the route north [cite: 07_riddle_cave_of_the_ash_tongue.md].

## Tactical & Narrative Markers (8–18)
These markers track the movement of entities and provide connective tissue for the DM.

* **8: Owlbear Patrol Circuit** Overlap of three-toed prints marking the beast's regular, territorial patrol between its hollow and the road [cite: 06_game_trails.md].
  
* **9: Gnome Warnings** Knotted trail cords (Gnomish "cables") used by mountain gnomes to communicate warnings about the beast and the light [cite: 06_game_trails.md].
  
* **10: Operative’s Trail** Narrow-soled boot prints belonging to a silk-wearing operative who moved north with deliberate steps [cite: 06_game_trails.md].
  
* **11: Scavengers’ Camp** A smothered campfire and bedrolls belonging to a group of rival scavengers from Critwall [cite: 06_game_trails.md].
  
* **12: K-Marks (Containment Symbols)** Precisely carved letters grown into tree bark that resolve into "K," acting as a containment diagram around key areas [cite: 04_dust_pool_glade.md, 05_thorn_choked_hollow.md].
  
* **13: The White Oak (Blackrazor Site)** The specific tree east of the main trail where the blade is driven into the heartwood [cite: 06_game_trails.md].
  
* **14: The Face-Cave Entrance** The center opening of the outcrop in Area 7, marked by stained stone and the smell of cold ash [cite: 07_riddle_cave_of_the_ash_tongue.md].
  
* **15: Spiral Chamber** The interior of the Riddle Cave where walls are covered in ancient geometric patterns and spiraling faces [cite: 07_riddle_cave_of_the_ash_tongue.md].
  
* **16: The Ash Tongue Mechanism** The stone face mechanism with a movable eye and tongue used to unlock the northern cache [cite: 07_riddle_cave_of_the_ash_tongue.md].
  
* **17: Northern Rise** Foothills marking the transition from forest to the basalt terrain of the mountain [cite: 07_riddle_cave_of_the_ash_tongue.md].
  
* **18: The High Road** The final exit path from the Thicket leading directly toward the entrance of White Plume Mountain [cite: 07_riddle_cave_of_the_ash_tongue.md].
