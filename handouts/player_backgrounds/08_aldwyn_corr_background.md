# Aldwyn Corr
### Human Paladin — Your Background

---

You have been with the Order of the Radiant Shield for eleven years. Squire at fourteen, border garrison for four years, three years as a travelling knight-errant following up on isolated threats across the Shield Lands interior, then two years assigned to the Order's investigative function. Not espionage — the quiet work of following up on reports from remote parishes about things that look like divine interference but aren't.

Your superior's instructions before this posting were direct: *"Find out if Keraptis is involved. If he is, find out how. If he isn't, find out what is using his name and why."*

---

### Why You Are Here

The reports that reached the Order described survivors carving the letter *K* into surfaces compulsively, often while muttering what onlookers described as a name. They did not remember doing it afterward.

The letter *K* is not a holy symbol. It does not belong to any deity in the Order's theological canon. And yet the compulsion it produces — the repetitive marking, the driven quality, the way victims describe it as an external pressure rather than an internal desire — bears a structural resemblance to divine compulsion that your training taught you to recognise.

Heironeous does not share names with dead wizards. The Order's position on this is straightforward. If something is imitating divine compulsion without divine authority, the Order needs to know what it is, where it is, and what to do about it. You were dispatched because you are the knight in the intelligence function with the most field experience in anomalous compulsion cases.

**Keraptis** is a name from history. A wizard of significant power, dead for over a century, associated with a mountain north of the Shield Lands. You have read what the Order's archives contain about him, which is less than you would like. The accounts are heavy on legend and light on methodology. What you know: he attempted something ambitious with mind-transfer magic. He is believed to have failed, or succeeded in a way that amounts to failure. He has been dead for a long time.

Whether something connected to him is genuinely active, or whether something unconnected is using his name, you do not yet know. That is what you are here to determine.

---

### The Coach Contract

You were in Ringstown when the coaches from Critwall were expected. You ended up riding on one for the last leg of road — the writ of passage from your Order gives you legitimate authority in Shield Lands territory, and Ranger-Captain Elnara found it useful to have you present for the delivery. The cargo was sealed. You were told it was sensitive. You performed a brief *Detect Evil* when the crates were loaded onto the second coach and noted nothing. Whatever is inside is not evil-aligned, at least from the outside.

---

### What Happened on the Road

When the creature came out of the forest, you felt it before you saw it. Not evil exactly — you can sense the shape of evil intent, the structured quality of a mind bent toward harm, and what came out of the Thicket was more like a pressure, a contamination, something that was not in its right mind because its right mind had been taken from it. The luminescence was wrong. The speed was wrong. The smell was wrong.

You survived. You are at the Ringstown gate. Elnara is standing there, and her expression tells you she has been expecting this outcome for longer than tonight.

---

### What You Know About the Region

The dust. People are carving *K* into surfaces after exposure. Three separate cases in the Order's reports before you were dispatched; seventeen documented cases in **Brother Aldric's** infirmary here in Ringstown. The compulsion is consistent across victims who have had no contact with each other, which means it is in the substance and not the rumour.

**White Plume Mountain** is north. It smokes. The local population is specific about not going near it. You have noted that the direction of the contamination and the direction of the mountain are the same direction, and you do not believe this is a coincidence.

You do not have confirmed intelligence about what is in the mountain. You have a working hypothesis and an order from your superior to confirm or deny it. The Thicket is in the way. You are not deterred by things being in the way.

---

### Who You Are

You are formal without being cold. You address people by name when you know it, by role when you don't. People have described this as distant. You consider clarity a form of respect.

You take the dust's compulsion effects seriously — not because you are afraid, but because you have theological training in the difference between genuine divine compulsion and its counterfeits, and the counterfeit quality of whatever is in that dust is, to you, a specific and deliberate offense. Something is imitating the sacred. You intend to find out what.

You will not execute a subdued enemy without due cause. You will stand between the party and someone they are about to kill in anger if that is what the situation requires. You have done this before. You have the scars to prove it eventually worked out.
