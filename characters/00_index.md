## Adventure-Specific Notes

**Whelm** (Area 2) responds to Wulfric by instinct. Any character can carry it; only a dwarf gets the full mechanical benefit.

**The cleansing rite** (Area 4) requires an anchor from outside the Thicket. Tessaly carries holy water. Brynn carries a waterskin of Ringstown well water. Either qualifies.

**The obsidian disc** (Area 3) can be identified by Mordechai with one round of concentration.

**Area 7's detection mechanism** is a Thief task. Kaida can disarm it cleanly; anyone else risks triggering it.

**The owlbear** (Area 5) is AC 3 and HD 8+3. Calder's longsword +2 and Wulfric's combat bonuses are the primary damage sources. Brynn's *Hold Animal* will not work on a dust-controlled creature but Mordechai's *Slow* will.

## What Each Character Knows at the Start

All characters arrive in Ringstown having survived the session zero event (the Coach B attack). They have:
- Seen the owlbear and know it is unnaturally large, fast, and luminescent
- Spoken briefly with Elnara at the gate
- No information yet about white plume dust, Sorel Vane, or the Inheritor's network

Individual hooks are noted in each character file.
