**Shortsword +1:** A narrow blade with a wrapped leather grip worn smooth from use. The balance is exceptional, it almost finds the target on its own, though that is probably just a metaphor.

**Ring of Protection +1:** Carved from dark bone, set in a thin silver band. Her Critwall client gave it to her before the job. She doesn't know where he got it and has stopped thinking about that.

**Dust Antitoxin (1 dose):** She acquired this in Critwall before departing, from a contact who knew more about the dust trade than he was comfortable admitting. Bitter grey tincture in a wax-sealed vial. +2 to the next saving throw against dust backlash effects.

## Background

Kaida has been working the Critwall information markets for six years, courier work, evidence recovery, the occasional quiet extraction. Her current client is a merchant factor she has worked for before, who became very interested in the white plume dust trade after one of his associates died from exposure. He wants names and a supply chain map. He is paying well. Kaida came to Ringstown via the same route as the coaches, which is how she ended up on one of them.

## Personality

- Watches everyone's hands. Occupational habit. She is not suspicious of the party specifically; she is suspicious of everyone equally.
- Will share information when it is tactically useful to do so. Withholds it when it is not. Considers this honest behaviour.
- Unsettled by deliberate, post-mortem markings on a body. Has seen a lot, but calculated cruelty reads differently to her than violence.
