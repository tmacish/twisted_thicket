Through fire and steam where the mountain bled,
Three shadows danced where the brave had fled.
With Trident of Tides and the Hammer of Might,
And the Soul-Drinking Blade, they fled into night.

The plume of white ash still chokes the sky,
Where the brothers watched the last dragon die.
But no steel remains in the cavern’s deep hold,
Only two names in the soot and the cold.

Northward they trekked past the shivering pines,
With winter’s own blood in their silver designs.
The relics are silent, the masters are gone,
Chasing the glint of a frost-bitten dawn.

So seek not the iron in the volcano’s breath,
For Marty and Bryce have cheated their death.
In a kingdom of ice where the sun never nears,
The Winters are kings of a thousand years.
